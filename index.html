Know about our Director

‚ùù

Our director Mr. Atanu Sarkar, is a Graduate and Diploma in Drama from Rabindra Bharati University, completed 6 month advance Theatre training under the guidance of Sangeet Natak Akademi, New Delhi. Founder of Thealight in 2001, directed about 25 long plays and 20 Short plays. And written 5 Plays.

National Scholarship awarded in 2000 & State Scholarship awarded in 2008. Participated in so many National & International Theatre Festival in India. In the Year 2008, participated at National Young Directors Theatre Festival, organized by Sangeet Natak Akademi, New Delhi in Patna. And in the year 2012, participated in International Theatre Festival, organized by Sambalpur University, Orisa at Sambalpur. In the year 2013 Thealight Participated in an International Folk Theatre Festival organised by Natya Chetana, Orisa, in Orisa.

Attending as a faculty in so many Actors & Director's Workshop. Directed plays in wide range of his style in various form of modern & mordern-folk Theatre. So many awards & prizes received in field of Direction.
